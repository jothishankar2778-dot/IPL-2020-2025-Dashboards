# IPL-2020-2025-Dashboards
Here’s a clean, parallel version of your project write-up adapted for an IPL dataset (2020–2025):

---

### **Project Title / Headline**

IPL (2020–2025) Interactive Dashboard using Power BI

---

### **Short Description**

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize Indian Premier League (IPL) data from 2020 to 2025. The dashboard provides insights into team performance, player statistics, match outcomes, and season trends using dynamic visuals and user-friendly filters.

---

### **Tech Stack**

Power BI Desktop
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query Editor
Microsoft Excel / CSV Files

---

### **Data Source**

Public IPL datasets (e.g., Kaggle IPL datasets)
Official IPL statistics from Board of Control for Cricket in India
Sports data platforms like ESPN Cricinfo

---

### **Features / Highlights**

📊 Interactive dashboard with slicers and filters
📈 Season-wise and match-wise analysis (2020–2025)
🏏 Team-wise and player-wise performance comparison
📉 KPIs showing:

* Total Matches Played
* Total Runs Scored
* Total Wickets Taken
* Win/Loss Ratios

---

### **Multiple Visualizations**

Line Chart
Stacked Bar Chart
Clustered Bar Chart
Area Chart
Pie Chart
Ribbon Chart
Waterfall Chart

---

### **📌 Dynamic Capabilities**

Dynamic DAX measures for real-time calculations
Interactive filters for season, team, venue, and player
Drill-down capabilities for detailed match insights

---

### **📌 Insights**

* Identified top-performing teams across seasons
* Compared batting and bowling performances of players
* Observed winning trends based on toss decisions and venues
* Highlighted consistent performers and emerging players

---

### **📊 Business Inputs & Insights**

🔍 **Business Inputs**

* Track team and player performance across IPL seasons
* Monitor strike rates, economy rates, and match outcomes
* Identify top run-scorers and wicket-takers
* Analyze venue-based performance trends
* Enable filtering by season, team, player, and match

---

### **Key Insights**

📈 Certain teams dominate across multiple seasons
🏏 Players show consistent performance trends over time
⚠️ Toss decisions can influence match outcomes in some venues
📊 High strike rates correlate with match-winning performances
📉 Bowling economy plays a key role in restricting opponents
